Start Your Career as an Apprentice

At RightStride Ltd., we believe in shaping the next generation of sock innovators. As an apprentice, you will learn every aspect of our unique business, from design and production to logistics and customer service. This is a chance to gain practical skills in a creative environment while being part of a fun and motivated team.

Required Skills

You do not need years of experience to join us as an apprentice, but we are looking for people who are motivated, curious, and eager to learn. Basic computer skills, an eye for detail, and good communication are important. You should be open to working in a team and willing to take on new challenges.

Special Requirements

We expect our apprentices to be reliable and to show initiative. You should be ready to follow instructions but also to share your own ideas. A willingness to try different tasks, from hands-on production to creative brainstorming, is essential.

Opportunities and Benefits

As an apprentice at RightStride Ltd., you will receive structured training, personal mentorship, and the chance to work on real projects from day one. You will also have access to our employee benefits such as flexible working hours, a friendly workplace, and occasional team events. Successful apprentices often move into permanent roles within the company after completing their program.
